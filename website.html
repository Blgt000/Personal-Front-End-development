Our Story

Two sisters. One table.

Founded in 2011 by sisters Liza and Maggie Hinman, The Spinster Sisters began as a simple idea: bring the farmers' market to the dinner table. What started as a cozy corner spot has grown into one of Sonoma's most celebrated dining destinations.

Every dish we serve traces its lineage directly to the soil of Sonoma County — heirloom vegetables, pasture-raised meats, and wildflowers from farms we know by name. Our menu changes with the seasons, because that is how it should be.
